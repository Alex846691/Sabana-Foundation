ABOUT SABANA

Web3 cybersecurity workspace and social impact foundation. Securing the decentralized world while channeling 100% of donations to charitable causes.

SABANA Foundation donates 100% of all direct donations and 51% of all earnings from audits and bug bounties to charitable causes
